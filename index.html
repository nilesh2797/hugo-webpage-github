Hi there 👋

I am a CS PhD Student at UT Austin advised by Prof. Inderjit Dhillon. My research interests include Large-Scale Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Web Search and Recommendation. Before joining my PhD, I spent 2 years at Microsoft Research India working with Dr. Manik Varma on algorithms and applications of Extreme Classification. I completed my undergraduate with Honours in CS from IIT Bombay.
